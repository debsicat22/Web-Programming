Stranger Things

Overview

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The series follows a group of friends as they uncover supernatural mysteries and government conspiracies, while facing the terror of the unknown.

Information

Stranger Things was created by the Duffer Brothers and premiered on Netflix in July 2016. The series has since become a cultural phenomenon and has received critical acclaim for its writing, direction, and performances.

Cast

The series stars Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Finn Wolfhard, Millie Bobby Brown, Gaten Matarazzo, Caleb McLaughlin, Noah Schnapp, and Sadie Sink, among others.

Reviews

Stranger Things has received widespread praise from both audiences and critics. The series has a 95% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes and has been nominated for numerous awards, including the Primetime Emmy Awards.
